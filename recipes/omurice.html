Omurice

Description

Omurice consists of an omelette made with fried rice and thin fried scrambled eggs, usually topped with ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 boneless, skinless chichek thigh
  • 1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed vegetable (defrosted)
  • freshly-ground black pepper
  • sea salt
  • 1 3/4 cups cooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • 1 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 Tbsp milk
  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 Tbsp shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Chop the onion finely
  2. Cut chicken into 1/2" pieces
  3. Heat the oil in a pan and saute the onion until softened
  4. Add the chicken and cook until no longer pink
  5. Add the mixed vegetables and season with salt and pepper
  6. Add the rice and break into small pieces.
  7. Add ketchup and soy sauce and combine evenly. Transfer the rice onto a plate and wash the pan.
  8. Whisk 1 egg and 1 Tbsp milk in a small bowl
  9. Heat 1 Tbsp olive oil in the pan over medium heat.
  10. Once the pan is hot, pour the egg mixture into the pan and tilt gently such that the pan is has a thin layer of the egg mixture
  11. Put 3 Tbsp cheese and half the ketchup friend rice on top of the omelette
  12. Use the spatula to fold both sides of the omelette toward the middle to cover the fried rice
  13. Hold the plate in one hand, the pan in the other and gently flip the pan and move the omurice onto the plate
  14. Drizzle the omurice with ketchup with desired pattern
