Tamago Kake Gohan

Description

Translated to English as "Eggs on Rice", this simple recipe is a classic Japanese comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked hot white rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon mirin (optional)

Steps

  1. Place rice in a bowl and make a shallow indentation in the center
  2. Break the whole egg into the center
  3. Season with soy sauce
  4. Add any optional seasoning if using
  5. Stir vigourously with chopsticks
  6. Serve immediately
